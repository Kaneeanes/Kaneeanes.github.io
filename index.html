Where I Live

I currently live in Hilton Head, South Carolina.

About Hilton Head              Hilton head is and Island off of the coast of South Carolina, that is home to many golf courses and a beautiful beach.

Places to Visit

There are many things to do on Hilton Head, and its one of the best vacation spots on the east coast.

Harbor Town

Harbor town is a marina home to many beautiful boats and the famous light house.

Atlantic Dunes

Atlantic Dunes is an amazing golf course that runs along the beach and is one of the top courses in the nation.

Hudsons Seafood

Hudsons is home to the best and most fresh seafood on the island. It comes in fresh from boats each day.

Shelter Cove

Shelter Cove is one of the best places to shop on the island and is home to the best ice cream.
